Where We’ve Been

GraceVine Community Church traces its beginnings to a small group of families who relocated with a shared vision to establish a purposeful and welcoming presence in our local community. Originating from a desire to cultivate relationships that go beyond mere acquaintanceship, our mission was clear: to transform friendships into a sense of familial connection. We endeavored to intersect our lives with those of our neighbors, offering care, concern, and ultimately sharing the hope found in Christ.

As time unfolded, our circle expanded. What began with a core of dedicated individuals grew steadily, from an initial eight to a thriving community of twenty-five, with each passing year witnessing further growth. Our commitment deepened as we invested in homes within the community, aiming to establish a lasting presence. It was during this period of reflection that we pondered the idea of creating a worship gathering, a spiritual cornerstone in the heart of our community. The vision of planting a church took root, and GraceVine Community Church emerged as the answer to that calling.

Where We’re Headed

In the vibrant tapestry of our community, every neighborhood within a 2-mile radius of GraceVine Community Church boasts a thriving collective of 20+ members who are purposefully engaged in serving, gathering, and connecting with one another and their immediate neighbors. These individuals not only provide leadership and direction to address community needs but also take proactive steps to extend invitations, welcoming others to join them in their meaningful work.

In the ongoing journey, new leaders consistently emerge, identified through their faithfulness and the fruitfulness of their efforts. the members of GraceVine Community Church become an increasingly vibrant local witness, elevating expectations for God to move and contributing to the broader spread of the Gospel in our community.
